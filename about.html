Welcome to FoodDan

Welcome to FoodDan, a donating platform that connects donors to verified nonprofits. We help you become a ray of hope for people in need. Choose a cause that is close to your heart and join hands with millions of donors like you who aim to make this world a better place.


We are a team of passionate individuals committed to addressing the issue of food waste in India. Our goal is to create a system that connects food donors with charities and NGOs, while also reducing the environmental impact of food waste.


At Food Dan, transparency and accountability are our core values. We ensure that every donation reaches its intended recipients and make regular updates to keep our donors informed about the impact of their contributions.


Join us in our mission to make a difference in the lives of those in need and create a brighter, greener tomorrow for generations to come.
