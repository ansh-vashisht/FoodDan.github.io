The need for food

1) People facing hunger due to poverty
2) Families affected by natural disasters
3) Individuals experiencing homelessness
4) Children in need of nutritious meals


Food Tips

1) Donate non-perishable food items.
2) Avoid donating expired or damaged food.
3) Include nutritious items like canned vegetables and whole grains.
4) Consider dietary restrictions of those in need, such as providing gluten-free or vegetarian options.

Who you could Help

1) Families struggling to put food on the table.
2) Individuals experiencing food insecurity due to financial challenges.
3) Children who rely on school meals for their primary source of nutrition.
4) Elderly individuals on fixed incomes who may face difficulty affording food.
